# AI Basics Workshop Proposal (Class 0)
**Duration:** 75 minutes (45 min instruction, 30 min practice)  
**Audience:** Non-technical or mixed-skill teams who want practical AI fluency fast  
**Format:** Live instruction + live demo + guided practice + Q&A  
**Instructor Needs:** Projector/screen share, reliable internet, instructor laptop with AI accounts ready

---

## Purpose & Outcomes
Equip participants to use AI safely and effectively on real tasks immediately after the session.
- Understand what today’s AI can and cannot do for them
- Create and refine basic prompts through conversation
- Apply AI to their own work/life tasks (research, planning, writing, brainstorming)
- Know privacy/safety guardrails and free vs paid tradeoffs

---

## Outline (75 minutes)
1) Opening: Who is AI for? (5 min)  
   - Interactive poll + shared belief: AI is for everyone with a structured path.
2) The 5 Levels of AI Enlightenment (7 min)  
   - Level 0–4 overview with one-line outcomes; today’s focus on Level 0 → 1.
3) Live Demo: AI as Research Partner (15 min)  
   - Real conversation shown live: research → iterate → organize → critique.
4) Safety & Smart Choices (10 min)  
   - What never to share; rule of thumb; free vs paid side-by-side.
5) Guided Hands-On (20 min)  
   - Shared first prompt, then open practice on participants’ real tasks with instructor coaching.
6) Share-Outs (5 min)  
   - 3–4 volunteers show results; discuss what worked and surprises.
7) Wrap & Homework (8 min)  
   - Daily use challenge, try multiple AIs, decision on subscription, bring back best/worst examples.

---

## Participant Requirements
- Laptop and internet access
- Account on at least one AI platform (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity); sign-up guide provided
- A real task to work on (planning, writing, research, brainstorming)

---

## Materials Provided
- Slide/visual: 5 Levels of AI Enlightenment
- Safety checklist (what never to share)
- “50 things to try with AI” starter list
- Homework tracking sheet (daily log + free vs paid decision)
- Links to AI providers and quickstart prompts

---

## Success Metrics
Participants leave able to:
- Hold a basic, safe AI conversation and refine prompts
- Explain safety guardrails and free vs paid differences
- Use AI on at least one real personal or work task during the session
- Feel confident progressing to “Level 1: Smart Usage” in the follow-up class

---

## Why This Works for Your Organization
- Fast lift: No technical background required; ready-to-use examples
- Safety-first: Clear privacy guardrails to reduce risk
- Immediate ROI: Participants solve a real task during the workshop
- Laddered path: Sets up the next sessions (Smart Usage, AI Ecosystem, Personal Automations, Build Stuff)
